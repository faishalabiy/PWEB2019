Food~


1. Krawu Rice



Krawu rice is a culinary that is very popular among the people of Gresik. Served using banana leaves makes it even more delicious when eating this one food. The fluffier rice served with stew, beef slices, beef viscera and chili paste, is a complete mix and is very tempting to taste. Try it!

2. Jubung



Well, this one snack is really suitable for souvenirs from Gresik. The jamb is made of black sticky rice on top of which is sprinkled with sesame, wrapped in areca nut skin. Well, imagine how it feels right?

3. Pudak



Pudak is made from rice flour, brown sugar / coconut sugar and coconut milk wrapped in areca nut fronds. Do not go too far to taste this unique culinary huh.

4. Otak-otak Bandeng



The name is Otak-otak bandeng also a culinary that you must try when you go to Gresik. Suitable for side dishes with it's soft bone. It's not hard to find this food in this town.
