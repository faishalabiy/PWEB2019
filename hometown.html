Welcome to my hometown

As you have known this town named Gresik located in East Java and it's excatcly next to Surabaya, we can reach there for an hour of drive. I was born there so it's basically can be called as my hometown right? there is my grandparents house, i always come visit them every i had a time. So many factory from many big company was built there such as Petrokimia, Well-known Semen Gresik and many more. There is also have a dock where we can see many fisher boat and also the big ship. The city itself is a seaside so it has many of pond that called tambak and many beach. it's not a very big town but it's a city with it's own comfort. You haven't been there?Dude, you must visit there sometimes and taste the grilled fish, really.
